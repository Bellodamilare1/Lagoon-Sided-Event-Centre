    Welcome to LagoonSide Events Centre

    Lagoon Side Event Centre is a permanent structure built on an ample expanse of land. It is an event hub built for Corporate events such as Annual General Meetings (AGMs), Award Presentation Ceremonies, Dinner Parties, Send-off Parties, Product Launches, Conferences, Seminars, Workshops, Training, etc and Private Ceremonies such as Birthday, Wedding, End-of Year Parties, Anniversary, Funeral Parties, Etc. The Event Centre is strategically located at a serene environment close to Ojo Lagoon Jetty and accessible from every part of Ojo Town, Lagos, where guests feel at home right from the entrance as they savour the cool breeze that oozes around the place while their stay lasts.
